My personal thinking style would have to be a majority of half Abstract Sequential (AS), and the other 75 percent Abstract Random (AR) with a few characteristics of about 25 percent Concrete Sequential (CS).

DBC can help me with my learning style since, I learn best when I have access to experts or references and also when I am placed into a stimulating environment. I also enjoy my point to be heard and I feel that DBC would allow me to have my own voice, and I would apply my logic to solve or find solutions to the problems that are in front of me.

Since I am also a Abstract Random (AR) I do also feel that DBC will aid in an environment where it is very personalized and personable, but also allowing the instructions or guidelines to be broad so I can interpreted as I see fit. Having a smaller class size will also aid me in being able to participate in group activates, since I also am a personable I also like to establish healthy relationships with others by listen to them and bringing my own harmony to group situations.

Since beginning of Phase 0, my biggest struggle would have to be just learning everything that I am doing and retaining that information and making sure that I can use it for a later time. I feel as if it is related to the way I think, I learn best when I am in groups, or when I am surrounded in a stimulated learning environment with the aid of professionals or experts that can help me with my issues.

I feel that now knowing more about myself and my learning styles I can make some more changes to the way that I handle everything. Changing my environment and doing more peer pairing so that I can learn from others and use my group skills to really help me out.
